# Inference

**Inference** is a opensource data anaylitic and managing tool. Allowing you to seamlessly connect your *databases*, *servers*, and *containers* to gether to create a unified view of your data. It all operates locally, consuming minimal resources and keeping your data secure. It's made for startups, hobbists, and enterprises. Its built with Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and TypeScript. Get valuable insights on your data with our beautiful and intuitive interface. Our tools is able to monitor your application 24/7 and alert you when something goes wrong. With our built in **webhook system**, Inference is able to notify you of any issues that may arise on your favourite platform. 

## Features

- **Connect to your databases**: Connect to your databases and get valuable insights on your data. 
- **Monitor your application**: Monitor your application 24/7 and get alerted when something goes wrong. 
- **Webhook system**: Get notified of any issues that may arise on your favourite platform. 
- **Beautiful and intuitive interface**: Get valuable insights on your data with our beautiful and intuitive interface. 
- **Operates locally**: Operates locally, consuming minimal resources and keeping your data secure. 
- **Open source**: Open source, free to use and modify. 

## Getting Started

Clone this repository within the desired folder.
`git clone https://github.com/NoahWhiteson/Inference.git`

Navigate to the folder.
`cd Inference`

Install the dependencies.
`npm install`

Run the development server.
`npm run dev`

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) to view it in your browser.
